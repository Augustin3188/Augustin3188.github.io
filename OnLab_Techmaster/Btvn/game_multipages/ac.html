Assassin's Creed: Valhalla

Assassin's Creed Valhalla is an action role-playing video game developed by Ubisoft Montreal and published by Ubisoft. Set in 873 AD, the game recounts a fictional story during the Viking invasion of Britain. The player controlsEivor, a Viking raider who becomes embroiled in the conflict between the Brotherhood of Assassins and the Templar Order.
